🔔 Push Notifications

📱 Enabling push on your device

AirAlert uses your browser's built-in push system — no app store download needed. You need to grant permission once per device.

  • 1
    Open Settings Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner of the app.
  • 2
    Scroll to Push Notifications Find the Push notifications section near the bottom of Settings.
  • 3
    Tap "Turn on push for this device" Your browser will show a permission prompt — tap Allow.
  • 4
    Send a test Tap Send test notification to confirm everything is working.

iPhone users: Make sure you're using Safari and that Notifications are allowed in iOS Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Settings for AirAlert. AirAlert must be added to your Home Screen for push to work on iOS.

⏰ Daily schedule & reminder hour

AirAlert doesn't fire episode alerts the instant a show airs — it runs a daily pass at a time you choose. This keeps things predictable and lets you set it for your evening routine rather than getting pinged at 3 AM.

In Settings → Schedule & nudges, set your Daily reminder hour to a local hour (e.g. 18 = 6 PM). AirAlert will check all your subscribed shows once per day at that hour and send a single bundled alert if anything aired that day.

Tip: Make sure your timezone is set correctly in Settings → Time & timezone first — the daily hour is always in your local time.

👀 Still watching? nudge

If an episode sits unwatched in Tasks for a while, AirAlert can give you a gentle nudge. In Settings → Schedule & nudges, enable the "Still watching?" toggle and pick a delay:

  • –
    1 day after air Good for keeping up with fast-moving shows.
  • –
    3 days after air Balanced — gives you the weekend but keeps you on track.
  • –
    7 days after air Relaxed — one nudge per episode per week max.

At most one nudge fires per episode, and only if the episode is still unchecked. If you mark it watched first, the nudge never fires.

📺 Per-show alert settings

You can mute all alerts for a specific show without touching your global settings. This is useful for shows you've fallen behind on.

  • 1
    Open the show Go to My List and tap the show.
  • 2
    Open Community tab Scroll to Watch progress & blur.
  • 3
    Enable "Binge-watching later" This mutes all episode alerts and blurs all episode threads for that show until you turn it off.

🔧 Not receiving notifications?

Work through these checks in order:

  • 1
    Check browser permission In your browser settings, confirm notifications are Allowed for the AirAlert site — not blocked or set to Ask.
  • 2
    Check OS-level permission On iOS: Settings → Notifications → Safari → ensure AirAlert is allowed. On Android: Settings → Apps → Browser → Notifications.
  • 3
    Check your timezone Settings → Time & timezone — if this is wrong your daily hour fires at the wrong time.
  • 4
    Re-subscribe to push In Settings, tap Turn on push for this device again. If it says already enabled, try a different browser.
  • 5
    Send test notification Tap Send test notification in Settings. If this arrives but episode alerts don't, the toggles are likely off.

iOS note: Push only works on iOS if AirAlert is installed as a Home Screen web app (tap Share → Add to Home Screen in Safari). Push does not work in a regular Safari tab on iPhone.
