      Quick start
      
          
        
      
        
      
          
      
            Airalert opens on Add Shows. Open this guide anytime with the ? button in the header. The home icon opens Your Profile; use the tabs below to switch sections.
          
      
          
        
            
      1. 
              Search and subscribe. On Add Shows, type a title — results update as you type, or press Enter for a full search (then Recent searches chips may appear). Trending shows suggests popular titles in your genres. Tap Subscribe or Add show on a card. Your list is on My List. Tap a title there for episode details.
            
        2. 
            
      2. 
              Your profile card. On Your Profile, add a photo, display name, and use Save name & photo. Open Reminders & timezone to set timezone and reminder hour, then Save schedule & timezone.
            
        3. 
            
      3. 
              Recommended for you. Still on Add Shows, expand Recommended for you below (use Refresh if needed). Suggestions are weighted by genres from your subscriptions (plus shared networks when needed).
            
        4. 
            
      4. 
              Tasks. Open the Tasks tab: when an episode airs on its calendar day, it appears as a checklist item. Check it off when you’ve watched. Optional: on Your Profile, set a follow-up push (1, 3, or 7 days after air) if you still haven’t marked it watched — e.g. “Have you watched … yet?!”
            
        5. 
            
      5. 
              Optional — Push. On Your Profile, use Turn on push for this device. On iPhone, add Airalert to the Home Screen first for reliable delivery.
            
        6. 
            
      6. 
              Refresh data. On Add Shows, use Refresh Data to pull TVMaze episode dates into the cache for My List and tasks.
            
        7. 
            
      7. 
              Own list per phone. If two devices show the same subscriptions unexpectedly, on one device use New list on this device under Reminders & timezone (clears this browser’s session).
