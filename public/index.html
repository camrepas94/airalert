                    Quick start

                    Airalert helps you track what’s airing, check off what you’ve watched, and hang out with other fans. You land on Add Shows first. Use the header for everything else: ? this guide · gear settings · person your profile · inbox (top right) when you’re signed in.

                    Build your list

                    On Add Shows, search by title (live results as you type, or press Enter for a deeper search). Recent searches appear as chips. Trending surfaces popular streaming shows that are airing; Recommended for you (expand the section) mixes genres, show type, and what similar fans watch — tap Refresh anytime.

                    Tap Subscribe or Add show on a card. Open My List to see what you’re following, tap a title for episode details, or check Binge later to mute air-day alerts and hide episode-thread spoilers until you’re caught up.

                    Guide & tasks

                    My week is a Sun–Sat grid of what’s airing from your list in your timezone. Tasks is your checklist: when an episode airs on its day, it shows up — mark watched, skipped, or leave it open. Completed episodes fold into Completed so the list stays tidy.

                    Community

                    Each show has General discussion plus a thread per episode. Tap + to pick a show and jump to a thread. You can follow thread notifications (push) when you’re on the show’s list or after there’s activity.

                    • Spoiler-safe mode — Set “episodes behind” or turn on Binge later to blur posts you’re not ready for; tap to reveal. Authors can still tag spoilers.
                    • Polls & scores — Vote in episode prediction polls before air; rate episodes 1–5 after. Season charts live in the general thread.
                    • Live rail — Tap I’m watching now during a live window to chat in the side rail with others there.
                    • Watch challenges — Community goals (e.g. finish a show before a finale airs). Opt in and climb the leaderboard; progress uses the same episodes you mark watched on Tasks.
                    • Find a friend — Search usernames and open profiles. Signed-in viewers see a compatibility % from shared list overlap and matching episode ratings.

                    People you follow

                    On My List, search TVMaze people (cast, writers, directors) and tap Follow. When a new show appears on their credits, you can get a push — toggle under Settings → Push notifications (People you follow).

                    Messages

                    Registered members can direct message from the inbox (top right) or from someone’s profile. Create an account under Your profile if you don’t have one yet.

                    Profile & settings

                    Your profile (person icon): photo, display name, optional about-you fields, Share your stats (PNG card for social), account, sign out. Settings (gear): timezone, Refresh all show data (updates air dates for your shows), and Push notifications — turn on the device, then choose which alerts you want (airs today, DMs, community, task nudges, people you follow, and more).

                    Tip: On iPhone, add Airalert to your Home Screen before enabling push for the most reliable notifications.

                    Settings

                    Refresh data

                    Update TVMaze episode dates in the server cache so My List air dates and Tasks stay current. New shows refresh automatically when you add them.
