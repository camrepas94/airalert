Airalert opens on Add a Show. This guide lives on Your Profile — use the tabs at the top to switch anytime.

  1. Search and subscribe. On Add a Show, type a title — results update as you type, or press Enter for a full search (then Recent searches chips may appear). Tap Subscribe on a result card. Use × in the field to clear and search again. Your list is on Your Shows (expand Your shows there to see it). To browse episodes for a show you already follow, tap its title in that list to open details.
  2. Save your profile. On Your Profile, set timezone and reminder hour (local), then Save profile. That drives correct dates, the server job, and your calendar feed.
  3. Recommended for you. Still on Add a Show, expand Recommended for you below the results (use Refresh if needed). Suggestions improve after you’ve subscribed to a few series.
  4. Optional — Push. On Your Profile, use Turn on push for this device. On iPhone, add Airalert to the Home Screen first for reliable delivery.
  5. Optional — Calendar. On Your Profile, copy the calendar URL under Calendar sync (Copy URL) and subscribe in Google, Apple, or Outlook. It updates when the app refreshes episode data.
  6. Refresh and log. On Your Shows, use Refresh Data (under Upcoming) to pull TVMaze data into the cache. Notification log lists what the hourly job recorded when an episode airs “today” in your timezone (entries are stored as dry-run; web push also fires when you’ve enabled it and the server sends a notification).
  7. Own list per phone. If two devices show the same subscriptions unexpectedly, on one device use New list on this device in Your profile (clears this browser’s session).

Still seeing the same shows on two phones? Tap here on one of them after updating the app — it clears this phone’s session so it can get its own list. (iCloud can sync website data; we no longer trust that for login.)
