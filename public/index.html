Quick start

Track shows from TVMaze, get reminders, and optionally sync your calendar. Do this once to get going:

  1. Save your settings. Set timezone and reminder hour, then Save profile.
  2. Add shows. Open Add a Show, search, open a result, and subscribe. Your list lives under Your Shows.
  3. Browse suggestions. On Add a Show, scroll below search to Recommended for you (appears after you follow a few series).
  4. Optional: Turn on Push notifications on this tab for this device. On iPhone, add Airalert to your Home Screen first.
  5. Optional: Copy Calendar sync and subscribe in Google, Apple, or Outlook.
  6. On Your Shows, tap Refresh episodes to update schedules. Notification log shows what the app recorded (dry run for now).

Add a show

Show title is matched against TVMaze (names only). The public TVMaze search API only returns about ten hits per request, so we also scan their full show catalog for titles containing your words — that way broad terms like “housewives” can list every franchise, not just the first few. Results are sorted by most recently aired when TVMaze provides it. Use Filter by network to narrow the list. Shows already on your list are hidden.
