📋 Tasks & Checklist

How episodes land in Tasks, how to mark watched, use binge mode, rate episodes, and keep your checklist accurate.

On this page

✅ What Tasks is for

The Tasks tab is your day-to-day watch queue. Episodes that have already aired show up here so you can mark what you've seen, skip what you're dropping, and rate what you finished.

Use My List to subscribe to shows; use Tasks to work through what's on your plate.

📅 When episodes appear

Only episodes with an air date on or before today appear in Tasks. Future episodes stay off the list until they've aired.

If something's missing after air day, try Settings → Refresh all show data to pull the latest schedules from TVMaze.

Timezone matters: "Today" follows your account timezone in Settings → Time & timezone.

👆 Watched, not watching & ratings

Each row is an episode. Use the in-row actions to:

  • •
    Watched Marks the episode complete and updates your progress.
  • •
    Not watching Removes it from your active queue without necessarily marking prior episodes.
  • •
    Rate After watching, you can leave a score where the app offers ratings.

Exact labels match what's shown in your beta build — they may appear as chips or buttons under each title.

🙈 Binge-watching & spoiler blur

If you're behind on a show, open the show → Community tab → Watch progress & blur.

  • 1
    Binge-watching later Mutes episode notifications for that show and blurs episode threads until you're caught up.
  • 2
    Episodes behind Optionally set how many episodes you're behind — AirAlert blurs only those threads until you reveal them.

Tap a blurred thread when you're ready to read — you're always in control.

📊 Checklist stats

The checklist summary (shows on watchlist, episodes to watch, watched count, etc.) updates as you interact with Tasks. Stats are there to give you a quick snapshot of how much you've cleared versus what's left.

🔄 Refreshing show data

Networks sometimes shift air times or episode orders. If Tasks looks stale, go to Settings and run Refresh all show data to reconcile with TVMaze.
