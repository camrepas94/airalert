💬 Community & Chat

Episode threads, spoiler safety, fan predictions, reporting, following cast, and how Inbox fits in.

Unlocking Community & Inbox Episode threads & posts Spoiler blur & predictions Reporting & safety Following cast & crew Inbox & direct messages

🔓 Unlocking Community & Inbox

Add at least 3 shows to your list via Add Shows. Once you've subscribed to three shows, Community, Inbox, and personalised picks unlock — as long as you're using a real account (not guest).

Why three? It keeps early beta discussion grounded in people who are actively tracking multiple shows.

🧵 Episode threads & posts

Each show has spaces for general chat and per-episode discussion. Open the Community tab on a show to see threads that match what you've watched — or what's blurred if you're behind.

Your @username is how people mention you. Keep it respectful; harassment and spam violate the Terms.

🙈 Spoiler blur & predictions

If you're not caught up, AirAlert can blur episode threads until you choose to reveal them. Configure this under Watch progress & blur on the show's Community tab.

Fan predictions let you vote before an episode airs; voting typically locks at air time and the author reveals results afterward — check in-app rules for the current beta.

🛡️ Reporting & safety

If you see abuse, spam, or content that breaks the rules, use the report flow in the app where available. Moderators may remove posts or restrict accounts that put the community at risk.

Reminder: Don't share passwords, payment info, or private details in public threads or DMs with strangers.

⭐ Following cast & crew

On a show page, scroll to cast/crew and tap Follow next to a person. AirAlert checks credits periodically; when TVMaze lists them on a new project, you can get a push — often before headlines catch up.

Manage these alerts alongside your other toggles in Settings → Push notifications.

📬 Inbox & direct messages

Inbox holds direct messages and notifications about replies or @mentions, depending on your settings. You need an account and (once unlocked) access to messaging features as enabled in your beta.

Push toggles for DMs and replies live under push settings — see the Push Notifications guide for detail.
