Data AirAlert Uses

  • Account details such as email, public username, display name, profile photo, and optional profile fields.
  • Show subscriptions, watch tasks, ratings, reviews, Community posts, direct messages, blocks, and moderation reports.
  • Notification preferences, web push subscription data, device/session information, and operational logs.
  • Beta feedback, waitlist submissions, and product analytics used to debug critical flows and improve the beta.

How Data Is Used

AirAlert uses this data to provide TV reminders, account recovery, watch tasks, Community features, moderation, notifications, beta support, and product reliability monitoring.

Controls

You can update profile details and notification preferences in the app. Signed-in users can delete their account from Profile → Session. Beta testers can submit feedback from Profile → Help & legal.
