FAQ

Common questions

How do I turn on push notifications? ▾
Go to Settings (top-right gear icon) → Push notifications → tap Turn on push for this device. You can then pick exactly what triggers an alert — episode air times, community replies, cast news, or direct messages. Each can be toggled independently.
Why am I not getting episode notifications? ▾
Check three things: (1) Push is enabled in Settings → Push notifications. (2) The Episode airs today toggle is on. (3) Your timezone is set correctly in Settings → Time & timezone. Episodes fire on the daily pass at the hour you configure — not the moment they air.
How do I sync my shows across devices? ▾
You need a real account (not guest). Open the Account menu (top-right person icon) → Create account. Use email + password or Continue with Google. Once signed in, your list syncs automatically across any browser or device.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▾
On the sign-in screen tap Forgot password? and enter your account email. A reset link will be sent when outbound email is active. If email isn't set up yet in this beta build, try signing in with Continue with Google using the same email address.
Why doesn't an episode show up in my Tasks? ▾
Tasks only shows episodes that have already aired. If the episode is upcoming it will appear after its air date. You can also refresh show data in Settings → Refresh all show data to pull the latest info from TVMaze.
How does binge-watching / episodes-behind mode work? ▾
Inside any show's Community tab, tap Watch progress & blur. Turn on Binge-watching later to mute all alerts for that show and blur every episode thread. Or set a specific number of episodes you're behind — AirAlert will blur only those threads. Tap to reveal any blurred thread when you're ready.
How do I report a post or member? ▾
Tap the ⋯ menu on any post, review, chat message, or member profile and choose Report. For member profiles, open the profile and use the report action at the bottom. Moderation actions go directly to the AirAlert admin queue.
Community and Inbox are locked — how do I unlock them? ▾
Add at least 3 shows to your list via Add Shows. Once you've subscribed to three shows, Community, Inbox, and personalised recommendations unlock automatically. You also need a real account (not guest) to post and send messages.
How do I follow a cast member or director? ▾
Go to any show page → scroll to the cast/crew section → tap Follow next to a name. AirAlert checks TVMaze credits hourly. When that person is listed on a new project you'll get a push notification — often before entertainment news covers it.

🗑️ Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account and all associated data — watch list, tasks, Community posts, messages, and push subscriptions — open the app and go to Profile → Session & destructive actions → Delete account. This action cannot be undone. Guest sessions (no account created) are cleared automatically when you clear your browser's site data for AirAlert.
