AirAlert Support

Private beta support and review-facing help entry point.

Open AirAlert

Get Help

  • Beta testers can send structured feedback from Profile → Help & legal → Send beta feedback.
  • Use Settings → Help, privacy & legal for privacy, terms, and account deletion guidance.
  • Use Community report actions for posts, reviews, chat messages, or member profiles that need moderation.

Account Deletion

Signed-in users can delete their account in the app from Profile → Session → Delete account. This removes the account and related AirAlert data.

Future Support Channels

Before public store submission, connect hosted support URLs and mailbox aliases such as support@airalert.app, bugs@airalert.app, and privacy@airalert.app.
