👤 Account & Sign-in

Create an account, sign in with Google, keep your list in sync everywhere, and recover access if you forget your password.

On this page

👋 Guest vs signed-in

You can browse AirAlert as a guest, but your list and settings stay only in that browser until you create an account.

A real account (email/password or Google) lets AirAlert save your shows, tasks, Community activity, and inbox to the cloud so they follow you to any device.

Community & Inbox require a signed-in account. Guests can explore some areas once other unlock rules are met, but posting and DMs need an account.

✉️ Creating an account

Open the Account menu (person icon, top-right) and choose Create account. You can use:

  • 1
    Email + password Pick a username, enter a valid email, and choose a secure password.
  • 2
    Continue with Google One tap if you're already logged into Google in the browser.

Your username is your public name on Community — pick something you're happy for others to see.

🔐 Continue with Google

Google sign-in uses your browser's Google session. If nothing happens, check pop-up blockers or try another browser.

If you already have an email/password account with the same email as your Google account, AirAlert may link or recognise them depending on your beta build — when in doubt, use one sign-in method consistently.

☁️ Sync across devices

Once you're signed in, open AirAlert on any phone, tablet, or desktop browser and sign in with the same account. Your subscribed shows, Tasks progress, and preferences sync automatically.

Tip: Push notifications are per device. Turn on push in Settings on each phone or computer where you want alerts.

🔑 Password & reset

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and enter your account email. You'll receive a reset link when outbound email is enabled for the beta.

If email delivery isn't available yet, try Continue with Google using the same email address you used to register.

Security: Never share your password or let anyone else use your account. You're responsible for activity under your login.

🪪 Profile basics

From your profile you can update optional fields like display name, bio, favourite show, avatar, and other settings offered in the app.

Anything marked public may be visible to other members in Community — check each field's description in the app.
