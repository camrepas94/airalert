🗑️ Delete account

How to permanently remove your AirAlert account and what happens to your shows, tasks, Community data, and push subscriptions.

On this page
Before you delete Steps in the app What gets removed Guest sessions Need help instead?

⚠️ Before you delete

Deleting your account is permanent. You will lose access to your watch list, Tasks history, Community posts, messages, follows, and notification subscriptions tied to that account.

This cannot be undone. If you're unsure, export anything important or contact support before proceeding.

📍 Steps in the app

Open AirAlert while signed in and go to:

  • 1
    Profile Open your profile / account area from the app menu.
  • 2
    Session & destructive actions Scroll to this section (wording may vary slightly by build).
  • 3
    Delete account Follow the prompts to confirm. You may need to re-enter your password or verify identity.

The exact path in your beta is: Profile → Session & destructive actions → Delete account.

🧹 What gets removed

Generally, account deletion removes data associated with your user ID, including:

  • •
    Subscribed shows and Tasks progress
  • •
    Community posts, comments, and votes attributable to you
  • •
    Direct messages and inbox threads (as implemented for your region/build)
  • •
    Push subscription for that account on your devices

Some retention may apply for legal, security, or backup reasons — see the Privacy Policy for details.

👤 Guest sessions

If you never created an account, you're browsing as a guest. There is no cloud profile to delete — clearing your browser's site data for AirAlert removes the local session.

How: Use your browser's settings to clear stored data / cookies for the AirAlert site or uninstall the Home Screen app on iOS.

💬 Need help instead?

If something isn't working and you're considering deletion for that reason, try Support → FAQ or email support@airalert.io first.
